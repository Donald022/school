FIRST Robotics - 801 Horsepower

Project Overview

Participated as a team member of FIRST Robotics team 801 Horsepower from August 2020 to December 2020. FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international high school robotics competition where teams design, build, and program robots to compete in alliance-based challenges.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Collaborated with team members on robot design and mechanical construction
  • Participated in programming and testing of robot control systems
  • Contributed to strategy development for competition matches
  • Worked in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment
  • Engaged in problem-solving and iterative design processes

Technical Skills Developed

  • Robotics programming and control systems
  • Team collaboration and communication
  • Project management under tight deadlines
  • Mechanical design and assembly
  • Testing and troubleshooting complex systems

Key Takeaways

This experience provided hands-on exposure to engineering principles, collaborative problem-solving, and competitive robotics. It fostered skills in technical communication, teamwork, and the ability to work under pressure while maintaining attention to detail and quality standards.
