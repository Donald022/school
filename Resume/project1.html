FIRST Robotics - 801 Horsepower

Project Description

As a member of FIRST Robotics Team 801 Horsepower in Merritt Island, FL, I collaborated with a team of students to design, build, and program competitive robots for FIRST Robotics competitions during the 2020 season.

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics challenges teams to design and build robots that can complete specific game tasks within strict time and resource constraints. Our team worked together to engineer solutions that balanced mechanical design, electrical systems, and software programming.

Key Contributions

  • Collaborated on robot design and mechanical assembly
  • Assisted with programming robot controls and autonomous functions
  • Participated in team strategy sessions and competition preparation
  • Worked under strict deadlines to meet competition requirements

Skills Learned

Through participating in FIRST Robotics, I developed and strengthened the following skills:

  • Mechanical Engineering: Learned principles of robot design, gear systems, and structural engineering
  • Programming: Applied programming concepts to control robot movements and autonomous operations
  • Team Collaboration: Worked effectively with team members across different specializations
  • Problem Solving: Developed creative solutions to engineering challenges under time constraints
  • 3D Modeling: Used CAD software to design and visualize robot components
  • Project Management: Learned to manage timelines and coordinate tasks to meet competition deadlines
